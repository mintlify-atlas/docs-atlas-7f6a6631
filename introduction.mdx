## What is Dayflow?

Dayflow is a **native macOS app** that watches your screen, analyzes it with AI, and generates a **timeline** of your activities with summaries. It's lightweight (25MB app size) and uses ~100MB of RAM and less than 1% CPU.

Stop guessing where your time went. Dayflow understands context - the difference between "researching on YouTube" and "watching cat videos" - so your timeline reflects real work, not just which window was open.

<Note>
**Privacy-minded by design**: You choose your AI provider. Use **Gemini** (bring your own API key), **local models** (Ollama / LM Studio), or **ChatGPT/Claude** (requires paid subscription). Nothing leaves your Mac unless you choose a cloud provider.
</Note>

## Why Dayflow?

Most time trackers only log app usage. Dayflow is different:

- **Context-aware tracking** - understands what you were doing, not just which app was open
- **Extraordinarily battery-efficient** - stays out of your way by design
- **Privacy-first** - data stays on your Mac; you control where AI processing happens
- **Open-source & extensible** - inspect the codebase, audit data handling, build it yourself

## How People Get Value

- **Founders:** See where days disappear, balance maker vs. manager time, and spot hidden context switching
- **Engineers:** Verify what really shipped, generate a standup recap in minutes, and reduce interruption drag
- **Students:** Make study sessions visible, reduce distraction drift, and build better routines
- **AI researchers:** See where time actually went across experiments vs. reading vs. coding
- **Freelancers:** Generate client-ready summaries, show proof of work, and catch scope creep early
