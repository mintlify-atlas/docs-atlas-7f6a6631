The Daily view provides a calendar-style visualization of your activities, organized by custom categories you define. It transforms your timeline into an actionable workflow overview.

<Note>
The Daily view requires an access code to unlock. This feature is currently in beta.
</Note>

## What is the Daily View?

The Daily view displays your activities in a grid format, showing:

- **Time blocks** from 9 AM to 9 PM (configurable)
- **Color-coded categories** for different types of work
- **Activity distribution** across your day
- **Workflow statistics** and insights

## Categories

Categories help you organize and visualize how you spend your time:

### Default Categories

Dayflow includes four default categories:

- **Work** (Purple) - Professional tasks and focused work
- **Personal** (Blue) - Personal activities and life admin
- **Distraction** (Red) - Context switches and interruptions
- **Idle** (Gray) - Breaks and inactive time

### Custom Categories

You can create and customize categories to match your workflow:

1. Click the **Settings** icon in the sidebar
2. Navigate to the categories section
3. Add, edit, or organize your categories
4. Assign colors to visually distinguish each category

## Standup Summaries

The Daily view can generate AI-powered standup summaries of your day:

### What's Included

- **High-level overview** of your activities
- **Key accomplishments** extracted from your timeline
- **Time distribution** across categories
- **Focus periods** and context switches

### Generating a Summary

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a date">
    Navigate to the day you want to summarize using the date controls
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Generate standup">
    Click the **Generate Standup** button at the top of the Daily view
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review and copy">
    Review the AI-generated summary and click **Copy** to use it in your standup or notes
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Standup generation requires an active AI provider (Gemini, Local, or ChatGPT/Claude) to be configured.
</Info>

## Workflow Statistics

The Daily view displays real-time statistics about your workday:

- **Total active time** - Hours spent on activities (excluding idle time)
- **Category breakdown** - Percentage of time in each category
- **Focus score** - Measure of sustained concentration vs. context switching
- **Peak productivity hours** - When you were most active

## Timeline Window

The Daily view shows a visual timeline at the bottom, highlighting:

- **Activity density** throughout the day
- **Category transitions** (where you switched tasks)
- **Gaps and breaks** in your workflow

Click any time block to jump to that period in the timeline.

## Use Cases

### Track Deep Work vs. Meetings

Use categories to distinguish between focused work and collaborative time, helping you balance maker vs. manager schedules.

### Identify Context Switching

Visualize when you switch between different types of work to identify interruption patterns.

### Prepare for Standups

Generate concise, accurate standup summaries in seconds rather than trying to recall your day from memory.

### Optimize Your Schedule

Review weekly patterns to identify your most productive hours and schedule important work accordingly.

## Access Code

<Warning>
The Daily view is currently in beta and requires an access code. Check with the Dayflow team for access.
</Warning>

Once you have an access code:

1. Navigate to the Daily view by clicking the **Daily** icon in the sidebar
2. Enter your access code when prompted
3. The feature will remain unlocked for your account
